Looking at how rain droplets create ripples in the water we can learn about hidden patterns and perhaps try to imitate them. In this project I used the ripple principle to create a three dimensional version of it in wood. The commission is for a lobby of a building where this natural form will provoke calm and a sense of tranquility. The solid Ash wood panels are CNC milled and the wood is left to show itâ€™s hidden inner rings and layers. This relatively new technique of working uses black and white imagery to generate the three dimensional surfaces.

##### Dimensions A: 180x90x9cm

##### Dimensions B: 90x90x7cm

##### Material: Ash wood, natural oil

##### Year 2020, In progress
